About Us

"A Hospital in search of new horizons in medical care with social commitment"


25 years have elapsed since we commenced our journey in the field of health care service. From the very inception, City Hospital has bestowed utmost care and dedication to the patients and clients. Turning back, we realise with gratitude that our clients had recognised our sincerity and dedication to the cause. Without their confidence and support we would not have become what we are today. From humble beginnings, the growth of City Hospital had been remarkable. We have bloomed into a multi – specialty, 300 bedded -hospital with all the latest and most advanced diagnostic and treatment facilities. City Hospital stands out as the only hospital in Malabar equipped with electro physiological studies (E.P. Study). Highly skilled and all- inclusive medical and surgical specialties is the hall mark of our hospital. Along with our well – renowned, traditional Gynecology department we have a fully equipped and advanced department for Cardiac and Thoracic ailments to undertake all types of investigations and surgeries.
